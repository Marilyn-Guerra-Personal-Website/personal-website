My Works of Art

Made on August 25, 2023

In this picture I drew a goddess praying. My inspiration came from Princess Zelda from the Nintendo game series "The Legend of Zelda". I tried my very best to practice capturing emotion in a still picture. Here you can see there is some worry but also a sense of confidence that her prayer will be answered.

Made on August 7, 2023

This one is more of a trial and error art piece that turned out really nice. While drawing I challenged myself to not use any refferences, just to draw from memory alone. I also wanted to get out of my comfort zone and draw a difficult pose. Thus where this piece came from! All I thought about when drawing this one was Spider-Man and outfits that a skateboader would wear.

Made on July 15, 2023

At the start of drawing this picture I had no idea how to draw wings. I have had many attempts that lead to failure in the past, but I was determined to try again. And, just like the last art piece, I decided to challenge myself by not using any references. Now that I have explained, without a doubt this is the piece I am most proud of thus far. The capture of emotion and the wings came out amazing, despite the fact that I did not use reference!
